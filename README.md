# NAVDIS — UPI Dispute Navigator

Find out if your bank has missed the RBI deadline on your UPI dispute.

**Live:** https://navdis.lovable.app

## What it does
Enter your bank, failure type, and dispute dates. Get your status,
your RBI rights, and a pre-filled escalation email — in 60 seconds.

## The key decision
AI explains. Rules decide. Day counts and status classification
are deterministic JavaScript — RBI constants in Object.freeze().
AI only generates the plain-language explanation and the email template.
Wrong information on Day 9 is financial harm, not a UX problem.

## Tech stack
TypeScript · React (TanStack Start) · Lovable · Supabase · Gemini 2.5 Flash
